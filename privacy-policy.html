Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-01

This Privacy Policy applies to the 4Pilots mobile application (“Application”), developed and provided by qubi / Milos Cubrilo (“Service Provider”).

4Pilots is provided as a Freemium service and is intended for use “AS IS”.

1. Overview

4Pilots is designed to respect your privacy.

The Application works fully offline and does not require the creation of a user account. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers.

Most information entered into the Application remains stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The Service Provider does not:

  • Require account registration
  • Collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Store flight plans on external servers
  • Use analytics tools
  • Track user behavior
  • Use personalized advertising
  • Sell or share personal data

Any contact information (such as email or phone number) that you enter into the Application is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the Service Provider.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The Application allows users to create and store:

  • ICAO flight plans
  • Aircraft profiles
  • Contact details (optional fields entered by the user)

This information remains on your device unless you choose to share it (for example, by exporting and emailing a PDF).

If you uninstall the Application, locally stored data will be deleted unless preserved through your own device backup system.

4. Advertising (Free Version Only)

The free version of 4Pilots displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • Advertisements are not personalized.
  • We do not collect or share personal data for advertising purposes.
  • The Service Provider does not receive personally identifiable information from advertising services.

AdMob may process limited device information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy. We encourage users to review Google’s Privacy Policy for additional information.

The Pro version of 4Pilots does not display advertisements.

5. In-App Purchases (Pro Version)

Purchases of 4Pilots Pro are processed exclusively through:

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play Store

We do not process, collect, or store payment information. All billing and subscription management is handled by the respective app store provider.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not sell, rent, or share user data.

We may disclose information only if required by law, such as to comply with a legal obligation or lawful request by public authorities.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable under local law).

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that appropriate steps can be taken.

8. Security

4Pilots is designed to minimize data collection and operate offline.

Since personal data is not stored on external servers, exposure risk is significantly reduced.

Users are responsible for securing their own device and managing device backups.

9. Aviation Disclaimer

4Pilots is a flight planning assistance tool intended for organizational and informational purposes only.

It does not replace official aviation procedures, regulatory requirements, or operational responsibility.

Pilots remain solely responsible for verifying all flight information and complying with applicable aviation authorities and regulations.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the Application after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Your Rights (EEA and Similar Jurisdictions)

Because 4Pilots does not collect personal data, most data protection rights are not applicable.

However, if you contact us directly and provide personal information (for example, via email), you may request access, correction, or deletion of that information.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
